About

Phy project is a basic language to simplify creation and control of physics engine on three.js project. Phy make bridge from three to physics in worker or directly. This is the compilation of all my physics work in javascript.

Phy provide same command on physics engine. Each engine have is own feature.

  1. OIMO is light, stable and speed for simple project. [link:https://github.com/saharan/OimoPhysics/ Made by Saharan]
  2. RAPIER is light, stable and speed for simple project. [link:https://github.com/saharan/OimoPhysics/ Made by Saharan]
  3. AMMO is wasm port of Bullet engine, is powefull and have soft body. Use for complex simulation. [link:https://github.com/kripken/ammo.js built by Kripken]
  4. PHYSX is wasm port of Nvidia Physx engine, is the most recent and powerful.It's the fastest and have very stable joint for robotics. [link:https://github.com/fabmax/physx-js-webidl built by Fabmax]
  5. HAVOK is wasm port of havok engine, industry standard used in majority of game. [link:https://github.com/BabylonJS/Babylon.js made with babylon team]
  6. JOLT is wasm port of jolt engine, a new engine very promising; full of innovative ideas. [link:https://github.com/jrouwe/JoltPhysics.js built by Jrouwe]

Rapier and Cannon are also in test...

Feature

Phy build provide loader, controler, environment, real character, particle engine, terrain editor, advanced geometry, instance geometry.
